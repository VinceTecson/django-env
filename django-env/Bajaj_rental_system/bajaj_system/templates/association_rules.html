General Guideline

General Rules

Bajaj Auto promotes safe and responsible riding practices.

  • Follow traffic laws and regulations at all times.
  • Association have a rules and agreement to be follow for the safety
  • Respect pedestrians, cyclists, and other road users.
  • Do not operate the vehicle under the influence of alcohol or drugs.

Vehicle-Specific Rules

In addition to general rules, please observe the following guidelines specific to Bajaj Auto vehicles:

  • Under association rules, since it is a rental unit, you must be careful while using the units.
  • Always check the unit's condition to avoid any incidents from happening.
  • Report any mechanical issues or malfunctions to authorized service centers.

Website System Rules

In addition to general rules, please observe the following guidelines specific to Bajaj Auto vehicles:

  • Customers are not allowed to create multiple accounts under one user.
  • Customer can only Rent one unit and can also create one schedule.
  • If customer have some issues or any problem contact directly to admin
